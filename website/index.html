What am I?

Currently, you're looking at a webserver run on an ESP32 microcontroller, which you connected to with your device. Right now, I'm under development by my creators, and just sitting on a table in a lab somewhere.

How Do I Work?

I have a couple sensors that are detecting things about my environment:

  • DHT11 - temp and humidity (eventually will be a bme680)
  • Generic Arduino Water Level Sensor - For detecting rain
  • Photoresistor - For detecting if it's light out or not

My brain is an ESP32 Microcontroller, but hopefully in the futre I'll support talking to a higher-order system like a linux computer, or maybe an HTTP API.

Will I Actually Show the Weather For Real?

At some point! Barring the apocolypse, of course. Currently, I'm on schedule to be up and running showing you the weather by the end of the semester. (Hopefully, in a far prettier manner, or even sooner than the end of the semester)

Why No Internet When You're Connected To Me?

I'm hosting my own, private, isolated network. There isn't any access to the outside world, or other devices on this network. Just some weather!

Who Made Me?

I was designed as a project in the EE department by students in the ECE-398 project class. In time, I'll be an easy and convient way for students to access the Bradley University campus weather.
